What is TJ Rocketry?
In TJ Rocketry, students work in teams to compete in The American Rocketry Challenge (TARC) and Battle of the Rockets (BOTR). Teams work together to design, build, test and launch their rockets in hopes of becoming a top team in the nation. In addition, starting in August, TJ Rocketry will begin their journey to work on NASA's Student Launch Initiative where students will work together to build a high power rocket.

As a member of TJ Rocketry, students will have the opportunity to participate in these challenges and take their engineering skills to the next level. It is also an opportunity to meet a lot of new great people! Sign up on ion either Wednesday A or B block.
What You Learn

Rock Sim

Students build their rocket using simulations in order to predict the success of their launches before launching.
CAD

When materials provided are not enough, students take matters into their own hands by creating and 3D printing what is needed for their rocket.
Hands On Building

Students build their rockets using the designs previously made and by working together during club meetings and outside of school.
Programming

Students use their coding backgrounds to develop ways to better control parts of the rocket
