SLI

NASA Student Launch Initiative is a research-based, competitive, experiential exploration activity. Starting in August, TJ Rocketry will be designing, building, and testing a rocket that travels between 4,000 and 6,000 feet. The rocket will carry a scientific payload and be able to land within 2,000 feet of the launch pad. This challenge is a nine-month long commitment where teams must send in proposals for an engineering design of the rocket which culminates in a final launch in Huntingsville, Alabama. We will post further updates and progress here.
