BOTR

Battle of the Rockets is a competition for experienced members of TJ Rocketry where teams build rockets that fly upwards of 1,000 feet into the air. TJ Rocketry participated in two events against other high school and college teams. The two events included a deployable payload and a mars rover challenge. TJ Rocketry placed highly on the national standing in both events.

Awards:
Deployable Payload: 1st Place
Mars Rover: 3rd Place
