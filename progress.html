Progress
TJ SLI is a team of high schoolers working towards the NASA Student Launch Initiative challenge. Follow our progress throughout the entire process.
The NASA Student Launch Team has been working since July 2022 and has made significant progress over the months.

  • 9th september, 2022

    Activity Fair

    Starting off the year with spreading the adventures and endeavors by recruiting new memebers to join TJ Rockery!

  • 19th september, 2022

    Proposal

    Completed our NASA Student Launch Proposal where we got a general idea of our Rocket and Payload along with a preliminary team.

  • 26th october, 2022

    PDR

    Completed and presented the PDR to NASA, took the constructive critcism and advanced onto the next stage.

  • 12th december, 2022

    Subscale Launch

    Scaled our main rocket down, and successfully simulated a launch to show that our rocket is capable of working.

  • 9th january, 2023

    CDR

    Presented our CDR to NASA and passed onto the next and final stage, continued to take feedback and construct the full scale rocket.

  • 7th february, 2023

    Weyanoke Family STEM Night

    Outreach at Weyanoke Elementary School, where we are spreading the work done by TJ NASA SL and TJ Rocketry.

  • 23th february, 2023

    Waples Mill Family STEM Night

    Outreach at Waples Mill Elementary School, where we are spreading the work done by TJ NASA SL and TJ Rocketry.

  • 26th february, 2023

    Vehicle Test Flight

    Vehicle Test Flight to ensure that our full scaled rocket is ready for Hunstville, Alabama

  • 6th march, 2023

    FRR

    Finished the FRR after months of hard work on Rocket, Payload, Finance and Outreach!
